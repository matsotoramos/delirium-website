Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 Aug 2025

Who we are

Tinamati (“we”, “us”) develops the Delirium app.

What data Delirium handles

  • Account & identifiers: player name and a party code for multiplayer sessions.
  • Gameplay data: party settings, player list, round results, scores, and similar session metadata.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and basic device information if diagnostics are enabled (e.g., via platform services).

How we use data

  • Provide and sync real-time gameplay and party management.
  • Ensure fair play (round setup, scoring, and history where applicable).
  • Improve stability and performance using aggregated diagnostics.

Legal bases (GDPR)

We process data to perform the service you request (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR). Diagnostics are processed under our legitimate interests in quality and security (Art. 6(1)(f)).

Sharing

We use cloud providers to deliver the app (for example, real-time databases). We do not sell personal data. Third-party services only receive data necessary to provide their function.

Retention

Game sessions and related gameplay data are kept for the duration of the session and limited history. Diagnostics follow provider defaults unless removed earlier.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, correct, or object to processing. To exercise these, contact us using the details below.

Children

Delirium is designed for general audiences and is not directed to children. If you are under the age required by your country, please play with a parent or guardian.

International transfers

Data may be processed in the EU and other countries where our providers operate, with appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email: privacy@tinamati.app.

Changes

We will update this page when our practices change. Material updates will be highlighted with a new “Last updated” date.
